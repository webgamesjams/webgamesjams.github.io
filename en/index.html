Webgames GameJam

Hey! What's going on? We're making games! WebGamesJam is a jam established by WebGames team! Every month we're crowding together to select an abosolutely random topic and make games in 2 hours.

Here are the rules.

  • The Jams is hold every pre-last Friday of the month.
  • Before Jam starts we randomly take 3 cards with features and 1 theme card from the Magic Deck.
  • Every participant chooses at least 2 feature cards of 3 and starts making a game. The theme is genertic for everyone.
  • In 2 hours the work stops and playable prototype should be shown. Everyone has fun :)
  • You can fix and polish your game on a weekend if you want, but adding of important game features is not allowed.
  • In the beggining of the next week we present the final versions of our games and start a vote!
  • The Winner goes to the wall of fame (and sometimes receives a motivating trophy).
